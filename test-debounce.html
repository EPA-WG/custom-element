Summary

The debounce mechanism ensures that:

  • Multiple rapid transform() calls are collapsed into a single execution
  • Attributes removed during merge and re-added in renderModel don't cause infinite loops
  • Responsiveness is maintained (1ms delay is imperceptible to users)
